KeyoAI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

This policy describes how KeyoAI (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use our iOS app and keyboard extension. It is provided for transparency and App Store disclosure. If you need legal advice for your situation, consult a qualified professional.

What we collect

  • Keyboard and text. Key presses are handled on your device. AI features may send selected text, prompts, or instructions to our servers only when you use those features. On iOS, network-based AI features require Full Access for the keyboard, as required by Apple for third-party keyboards that use the network.
  • Subscriptions. Purchase status is validated with Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your Apple ID password.
  • Technical data. Our servers may receive standard data needed to operate the service (for example, IP address for security and rate limiting, timestamps, and error logs). We do not use this for advertising.

How we use information

  • To provide AI-assisted writing, suggestions, translation, and related features you request.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the service (including abuse prevention and rate limiting).
  • To process subscriptions and restore purchases through Apple.

Third-party services

We use infrastructure and AI providers (for example, Supabase for hosting and authentication-related services, and AI model providers such as DeepSeek accessed through our secure proxy). Those providers process prompts and technical metadata as needed to fulfill requests. Their use is subject to their respective terms and privacy policies.

Data retention

We design the service so that message content is processed to deliver your request and is not kept longer than necessary for operation, debugging, and legal compliance. Exact retention may evolve; we will update this page for material changes.

Your choices

  • You can disable Full Access or remove KeyoAI in Settings → General → Keyboard on your iPhone. Some AI features may not work without Full Access.
  • You can stop using the app or delete it at any time.

Children

KeyoAI is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

International users

If you use the app from outside your home country, your information may be processed in countries where we or our service providers operate.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
